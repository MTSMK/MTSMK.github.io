About me

My name is Matias, and I'm a guitarist from Southern Finland. I've been playing guitar for about five years now, and during that time, I've explored a wide range of genres. While I enjoy blues, rock, and various other genres, I’ve always found myself gravitating towards more heavier stuff.

In the early stages of my journey, I took private guitar lessons to build a solid foundation. However, when the COVID-19 pandemic hit, I shifted to teaching myself. Since then I spent the rest of my high school years practicing, playing and performing with my friends, while also working on my own compositions.
