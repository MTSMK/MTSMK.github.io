Projects

Senior high school music diploma

As part of my diploma, I completed a performance-based project by organizing and executing a live concert. The diploma required not only musical proficiency but also skills in planning and coordination. I curated the setlist, which featured a blend of metal, rock, and an original composition, with performances of tracks like Metallica’s Blackened, Children of Bodom’s Children of Decadence, and John Mayer’s Slow Dancing in a Burning Room.

One of the key challenges was working with limited resources. From securing rehearsal space to managing the concert’s technical elements, including sound mixing and lighting, we had to be resourceful in overcoming constraints. Despite these limitations, the project allowed me to develop both my performance skills and my ability to organize a live event. The music diploma provided invaluable experience, pushing me to grow as a musician while managing the logistical demands of a successful concert.
