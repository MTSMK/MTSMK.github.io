Children of decadence (COB-cover)

Children of Decadence is one of the most enjoyable songs I’ve performed live. Its fast rhythms and intense guitar work made it a crowd favorite during my diploma concert. Having played this song with the same band previously, we were able to focus on perfecting the performance, resulting in one of the most polished and technically impressive moments of the concert.

Holy wars...The punishment due (Megadeth-cover)

This complex and technically demanding track was one of the highlights of my music diploma concert. The song showcases intricate riffs, challenging solos, and dynamic shifts, making it one of the most difficult pieces I've performed. It pushed my technical abilities as a guitarist, especially during the fast-paced solos, and it remains one of the most rewarding songs I’ve learned to play.
